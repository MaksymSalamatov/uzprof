Introduction to Forex trading

What is the Forex market: a brief history

We will tell you when the foreign exchange market appeared and How did Forex trading begin? We will introduce you into the theory of the market, they will tell interesting stories and facts from the life of famous traders, as well as introduce with the history of the formation and development of various types trade. You will learn about the principles of effective trading and understand the underside of foreign exchange transactions.

Market advantages

The Forex market is the largest market in trading. Annual the turnover of the Forex market is 4 trillion dollars, which makes it and the most popular trading place not only for experienced traders, but also for beginners. Its advantages are 24/7 market operation, low cost of transactions, lack of restrictions, and also in high liquidity and much more. It is this market allows you to make a profit quickly, simply and transparently.

How to start trading on Forex

It is worth starting Forex trading by defining your goals and opportunities and choice of deposit. Assess all the risks, find the amount, the loss of which, in the event of unforeseen risks, would not cause you panic and continue your first steps into the market. Next you have a choice broker: an assessment of how it suits you, whether it matches broker to your expectations, opportunities and other things, registration and creation accounts. After you can already make a deposit, then select an asset and conduct a market analysis. Our team will provide you with everything you need for a successful start in the Forex market - training, video tutorials, trading signals, many tools and much more.

The main mistakes of newcomers to the market

Beginning investors often make mistakes related to their psychological state, as they often succumb to negative emotions. That is why it is very important to take into account your condition, mood, as well as the feelings that you have while trading. Excessive fanaticism and greed can play a cruel joke on your account. Also, one of the most common mistakes in trading is overconfidence. In this block, we will clearly show how to avoid serious mistakes during trading.
